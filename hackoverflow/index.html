ABOUT US

IEEE Industrial Applications Society, a group of undergraduates at VIT, is dedicated to build a bridge between enthusiasm and excellence by providing a platform for brainstorming solutions to the industrial world, through the largest research conferences, expert talks, seminars, and boot camps. We link research to practice, thereby connecting students to industry experts. To promote technology among the students even during the pandemic, we organised a variety of productive technical sessions, Hackathons, Techathons, and Ideathons, as well as some other events.

ABOUT HACKOVERFLOW

IHackOverflow is a 48 hours hackathon which aims to provide hours of ideation and innovation. It is an event for hackers to challenge their technical boundaries by learning new technologies, developing innovative projects and procuring new-age solutions. This event intends to bring together like minded hackers from various colleges and universities for an amazing technical journey consisting of pre-hack webinars and workshops. The theme for this Hack is Ukraine-Russia war and is primarily based on 6 tracks namely Logistics, Defense, Fintech, Healthcare, Telecom and Media & Open Innovation. The teams will be divided into groups of 4. Domain experts from each track will judge and will provide guidance to the hackers to improve their solutions. The hackers will be awarded with special cash prizes and lots more

FAQ

+

How can we register for the event?

You have to apply on the graVITas website

+

What is the team capacity for the event?

2 members

+

Is this event open to students of all years and courses?

Yes, anyone with a knack for coding can participate in the event.

+

Is there a specific coding language required?

No, We do not have any restriction as far as coding language is concerned.

+

When is the deadline to apply for the event?

You are all good. You have until midnight on September 29th, 2022!

+

How long will the hackathon be for?

The Hackathon will take place over 3 days the 6th, 7th, and the 8th of January. This includes time for both round 1 as well as round 2.

+

What happens in the ideation round?

In the Ideation round the teams have to come up with an idea based on the problem statement they have decided to work on, as well as create a small presentation about it.

+

Where will the hackathon be hosted?

The hackathon is based on the DevFolio Platform. It will be a completely online event for all external students(i.e participants who are from outside VIT) and a hybrid event (i.e participants can come to an assigned classroom to collaborate together or can work together online) for students of VIT Vellore.
